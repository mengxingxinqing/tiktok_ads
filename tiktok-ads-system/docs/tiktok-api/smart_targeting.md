# Smart Targeting

Smart Targeting is an intelligent feature that helps you find the users most likely to complete your objective. When enabled, Smart Targeting will allow TikTok to deliver ads outside the designated Interest & Behaviors or Audience settings when it is likely to improve ad performance for the advertiser. Smart Targeting consists of Smart audience and Smart interests & behaviors.
- Smart audience: To improve ad performance based on your objective, creative and additional platform signals. Your audience selections will be used as inputs and expanded if it's likely to improve performance.  
- Smart interests & behaviors: To improve ad performance based on your objective, creative and additional platform signals. Your interests & behaviors selections will be used as inputs, and your audience will be expanded if it's likely to improve performance. 

## Supported advertising objectives
The supported advertising objectives for Smart audience and Smart interests & behaviors are:
- Traffic
- App Promotion with App promotion type as App install or App pre-registration
- Lead Generation
- Website Conversions

## How Smart Targeting works
1. Launch: We launch the ad with the exact targeting criteria you set.
2. Observe: Our model observes ad performance to determine if the ad is underperforming or if there are users outside the targeting audience that are likely to convert.
3. Expand: If we believe we can improve performance, our targeting model will expand the selected targeting settings to maximize campaign objectives.
  - Note: Smart Targeting will not affect other targeting variables that you have selected, such as age, gender, or location.
  
## How to enable Smart Targeting
To learn about how to enable Smart audience or Smart interests & behaviors for your ad groups, see [Enable Smart Targeting for your ad groups](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1783164826830849).
