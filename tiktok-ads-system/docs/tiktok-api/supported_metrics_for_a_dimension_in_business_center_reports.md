# Supported metrics for a dimension in Business Center reports

**Doc ID**: 1775747492072449
**Path**: Marketing API/Reporting/Guides/Report types/Business Center reports/Supported dimensions/Supported metrics for a dimension in Business Center reports

---

See the tables below to learn about the supported metrics when you use a dimension in Business Center reports. You can find out the detailed introductions of the metrics in [Business Center reports - Supported metrics](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775747476019266).

## Supported metrics for `advertiser_id`
All metrics listed in [Business Center reports - Supported metrics](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775747476019266) are supported for this dimension.
