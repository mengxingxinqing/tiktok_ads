A catalog is an asset that allows you to store information about the products you want to promote on TikTok. Using a catalog is a great solution for anyone who is looking for an efficient way to create and manage ads that feature a variety of products, like an online retailer. You can use feeds to fetch product information from your product database and update a catalog in your Business Center. Catalog Feeds API enables you to manage the feeds for a catalog under your Business Center.

See [Overview](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754263078057986) and [Guides](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754263100123137) to learn about the concepts and benefits of catalogs, and to find out how to create and manage catalogs.

This group consists of the following endpoints:
* [Create a feed](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740665161957377)
* [Get feeds](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740665183073281)
* [Update a feed](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740665197662210)
* [Delete a feed](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740665210863617)
* [Get the log of a feed](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740665225631810)
* [Update the schedule status of a feed](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1750349624999937)
