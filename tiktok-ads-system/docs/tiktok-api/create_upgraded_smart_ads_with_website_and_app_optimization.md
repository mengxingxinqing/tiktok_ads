# Create Upgraded Smart+ Ads with Website and App Optimization

**Doc ID**: 1854746404386113
**Path**: Use Cases/Campaign creation/Create an Upgraded Smart+ Campaign/Create Upgraded Smart+ Ads with Website and App Optimization

---

This article walks you through the steps to create Upgraded Smart+ Ads with Website and App Optimization using the Sales objective.

Website and App Optimization is available for businesses with both website and app channels. The feature is designed to unlock the full potential of user interest and conversion opportunity by combining both website and app audiences in a unified campaign strategy.

Based on your preference for using a catalog to promote your ads, you have the flexibility to create two types of ads with Website and App Optimization:

- **Upgraded Smart+ Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization**: This type of ad uses a catalog to promote your ads. For detailed instructions on creating such ads, see [Create Upgraded Smart+ Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855007137120578).
- **Upgraded Smart+ Non-Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization**: This type of ad doesn't use a catalog to promote your ads. For detailed instructions on creating such ads, see [Create Upgraded Smart+ Non-Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1855007163018689).
