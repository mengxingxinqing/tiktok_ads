# BC Reporting

**Doc ID**: 1775752272288770
**Path**: API Reference/BC Reporting

---

Business Center is a one-stop business hub for organizations of all sizes to activate marketing activities and reach desired audiences in a collaborative way on TikTok. BC Reporting API enables you to retrieve the supported values of the currency and place of registration filters that can be used in a Business Center report for a specific Business Center.

To learn about the supported dimensions, metrics, filters and use cases for Business Center reports, refer to [Business Center reports](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775747432712194).

This group consists of the following endpoint:
- [Get currencies and registration areas for ad accounts](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1775752357139457)
