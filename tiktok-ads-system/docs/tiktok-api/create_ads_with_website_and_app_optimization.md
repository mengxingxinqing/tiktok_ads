# Create ads with Website and App Optimization

**Doc ID**: 1839876593708034
**Path**: Use Cases/Campaign creation/Create ads with Website and App Optimization

---

This article walks you through the steps to create ads with Website and App Optimization.

# Introduction
Website and App Optimization is available for businesses with both website and app channels. The feature is designed to unlock the full potential of user interest and conversion opportunity by combining both website and app audiences in a unified campaign strategy.

**You can use Campaign Management API to create ads with Website and App Optimization, and this helps you streamline your ad creation experience, and elevate operational efficiency and scalability.**

# Prerequisites
- You've gained access to TikTok API for Business. See [Get Started - Step by step workflow](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1735713609895937) for details. 
  - To create ads with Website and App Optimization, you need relevant permissions. See [API Reference](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1735713875563521) to find out permissions required for endpoints (including the endpoints listed in the "Steps" section) and see [Update app permissions](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1738855280338946) to find out how to configure permissions.
 - Creating ads with Website and App Optimization is currently an allowlist-only feature. If you would like to access it, please contact your TikTok representative.

# Steps
Based on your preference for using a catalog to promote your products and the campaign type, you have the flexibility to create three types of ads with Website and App Optimization:
- Manual Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization: This type of ad is within a Manual Campaign and uses a catalog to promote your products. For detailed instructions on creating such ads, see [Create Manual Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839882947475458).
- Manual Non-Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization: This type of ad is within a Manual Campaign and doesn't use a catalog to promote your products. For detailed instructions on creating such ads, see [Create Manual Non-Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1839882969587906).
- Smart+ Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization: This type of ad is within a Smart+ Campaign and uses a catalog to promote your products. For detailed instructions on creating such ads, see [Create Smart+ Catalog Ads with Website and App Optimization](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1842232769643905).
