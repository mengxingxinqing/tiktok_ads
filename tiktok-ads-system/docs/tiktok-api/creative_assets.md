---

Creative assets refer to the multimedia elements presented to users in ads, such as videos and pictures. 

# Creative asset types
Creative assets have the following types:
- **Images**: See [Upload videos, images and music](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738963828459521) to find out how to upload images. 
- **Videos**: See [Upload videos, images and music](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738963828459521) to find out how to upload videos. 
- **Music**: See [Upload videos, images and music](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1738963828459521) to find out how to upload music. 
- **Ad texts**: Specify `ad_text` to convey the message you want to promote to your audience via [/ad/create/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739953377508354) or [/ad/update/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739953405142018). 
- **Instant Pages**: See [Instant pages](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1739953260466178&rid=8io5ucomqr7) to find out how to get an instant page. 
- **Call-to-action text** (CTA portfolio): See [Call-to-action texts](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1749018853745665&rid=8io5ucomqr7)  to learn more about CTA portfolio and recommendations.  
- **Interactive Add-ons**: See [Interactive Add-on](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1749019605861377&rid=8io5ucomqr7) to find out the supported types and how to use these add-ons in your ads.  See [Cards](https://business-api.tiktok.com/portal/docs?id=1749019652141058) to check out how to use different cards in ads. 

# Creative specifications
Creative requirements may differ based on ad placements. See the [Ad Formats](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article?aid=9629) chapter on TikTok Business Help Center for details.
