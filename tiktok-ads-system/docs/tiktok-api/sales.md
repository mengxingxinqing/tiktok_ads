# Sales

**Doc ID**: 1834189283112962
**Path**: Marketing API/Campaign Management/Guides/Campaign/Advertising objective/Sales

---

Sales is an advertising campaign objective to drive sales on your TikTok Shop, website, or app. The objective simplifies campaign setup by combining Website Conversions and Product Sales into a single objective, enabling you to drive and measure sales in a more intuitive way. See [About the Sales advertising objective on TikTok](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/sales-advertising-objective-tiktok) for more details.

Currently, the Sales objective supports the following sales destinations (`sales_destination`):
- TikTok Shop (`TIKTOK_SHOP`): Drive sales on your TikTok shop. 
- Website (`WEBSITE`): Drive sales on your website. 
- App (`APP`): Drive sales on your app (product catalog required).
