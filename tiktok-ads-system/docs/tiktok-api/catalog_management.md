# Catalog Management

**Doc ID**: 1740132866693122
**Path**: API Reference/Catalog Management

---

> **Important**
By using Catalog API endpoints, you certify that you are abiding by the [TikTok Advertising Policies](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/advertising-on-tiktok-first-things-to-note). Please review these policies and ensure that the items you upload to your catalog don't breach them.

A catalog is an asset that allows you to store information about the products you want to promote on TikTok. Using a catalog is a great solution for anyone who is looking for an efficient way to create and manage ads that feature a variety of products, like an online retailer. Catalog Management API enables you to manage catalogs in a Business Center and to migrate catalogs to your Business Center.

See [Overview](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754263078057986) and [Guides](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1754263100123137) to learn about the concepts and benefits of catalogs, and to find out how to create and manage catalogs.

Catalogs are now assets under the Business Center. You usually need to specify a Business Center ID when you use Catalog Administration API. If you have catalogs that are created under your ad account, you must migrate them to your Business Center and you can migrate by calling [/catalog/capitalize/](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740490222539778).

This group consists of the following endpoints:

* [Create a catalog](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740306481704961)
* [Update the name of a catalog](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740306544966657)
* [Delete a catalog](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740310064588801)
* [Get catalogs](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740315452868610)
* [Get the lexicon list for a catalog](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740488375815169)
* [Migrate a catalog to a BC](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740490222539778)
* [Get available regions](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740491257516034)
* [Get locations and currencies](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740491571747841)
* [Get the overview of a catalog](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1740492470201345)
