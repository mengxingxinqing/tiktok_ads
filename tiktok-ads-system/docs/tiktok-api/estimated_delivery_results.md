# Estimated Delivery Results

**Doc ID**: 1746463274713089
**Path**: Marketing API/Campaign Management/Guides/Ad group/Estimated Delivery Results

---

Estimated Delivery Results (EDR) is a feature that helps improve your ad group performance by providing bid and budget suggestions. Bid or budget suggestions are derived from historical delivery data and allow you to estimate performance for in-delivery ad groups under different bid or budget scenarios. With our estimated results, you can explore different bid or budget settings to maximize your spending efficiency and ads performance. 
 
>**Note**
**The recommendations are only valid on the day (advertiser account time zone) suggestion is received, and the estimated result is valid for the same day. **
 
 
- See [Get recommended budgets](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1746463333984258) to find out how to use budget EDR.
- See [Get recommended bids](https://ads.tiktok.com/marketing_api/docs?id=1746463347473410) to find out how to use bid EDR.
